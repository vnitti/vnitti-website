PPL Workout Generator

An online app that generates random workout routines following the Pull-Push-Leg principle.

The app offers the user the possibility of structuring their routine without too much planning and also of turning them in a daily challenge. Just set the type of routine you want, number of exercises and sets and the rests between them.

This apps also serves for tracking the routine as you work out, making use of the chronometer and the pannel.

It was made with vanilla JavaScript, HTML and CSS.
